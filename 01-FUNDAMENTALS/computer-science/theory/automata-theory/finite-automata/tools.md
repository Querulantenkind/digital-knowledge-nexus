## BEGINNER

### JFLAP
Author: Susan Rodger (Duke University)
Year: 2023
Platform: Java (cross-platform)
Link: https://www.jflap.org/
Description: Educational tool for creating, simulating, and converting finite automata.
Why included: Industry standard for teaching. Feature-rich and well-documented.

### Automata Simulator (Web)
Author: Various developers
Year: 2024
Platform: Web browser
Link: https://automatonsimulator.com/
Description: Browser-based DFA/NFA simulator with visual interface.
Why included: No installation needed. Quick experiments.

### Regex101
Author: Firas Dib
Year: 2024
Platform: Web browser
Link: https://regex101.com/
Description: Regular expression tester with automata visualization.
Why included: Practical tool connecting regex to automata.
