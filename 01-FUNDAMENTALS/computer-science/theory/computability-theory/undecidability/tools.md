## BEGINNER

### Halting Problem Visualizer
Author: Various developers
Year: 2024
Platform: Web browser
Link: https://github.com/topics/halting-problem
Description: Interactive visualizations of the halting problem proof.
Why included: Makes diagonalization argument concrete.

### Reduction Builder
Author: CS education community
Year: 2023
Platform: Web browser
Link: https://github.com/topics/computational-reductions
Description: Tool for constructing and verifying reductions between undecidable problems.
Why included: Learn by building reductions step-by-step.

## INTERMEDIATE

### Busy Beaver Calculator
Author: Research community
Year: 2024
Platform: Python/Web
Link: https://bbchallenge.org/
Description: Tools for computing and verifying busy beaver values.
Why included: Active research project. Concrete undecidable function.

### Undecidability Proof Checker
Author: Formal methods community
Year: 2023
Platform: Python
Link: https://github.com/search?q=undecidability+proof+checker

Why included: Verify your understanding with machine checking.

### JFLAP
Author: Susan Rodger (Duke University)
Year: 2023
Platform: Java (cross-platform)

Description: Educational tool including undecidability demonstrations.
Why included: Standard teaching tool. Widely used.
