## INTERMEDIATE

### On Computable Numbers, with an Application to the Entscheidungsproblem
Author: Alan Turing
Year: 1936
Publisher: Proceedings of the London Mathematical Society
DOI: 10.1112/plms/s2-42.1.230
Link: https://www.cs.virginia.edu/~robins/Turing_Paper_1936.pdf
Description: Turing's original paper proving the halting problem undecidable.
Why included: The birth of undecidability theory. Foundational paper.

### Classes of Recursively Enumerable Sets
Author: Emil Post
Year: 1944
Publisher: Bulletin of the American Mathematical Society
Link: https://projecteuclid.org/journals/bulletin-of-the-american-mathematical-society/volume-50/issue-5/
Description: Post's problem and the structure of r.e. sets.
Why included: Extends undecidability to degree structures.

### Gödel's Incompleteness Theorems
Author: Kurt Gödel
Year: 1931
Publisher: Monatshefte für Mathematik und Physik
Link: https://www.jstor.org/stable/2021199
Description: Gödel's original incompleteness paper, precursor to computational undecidability.
Why included: Shows limits of formal systems. Foundation of undecidability.

## ADVANCED

### Classes of Recursively Enumerable Sets and Their Decision Problems
Author: Henry Rice
Year: 1953
Publisher: Transactions of the American Mathematical Society
DOI: 10.2307/1990888
Link: https://www.ams.org/journals/tran/1953-074-02/S0002-9947-1953-0053041-6/
Description: Rice's theorem proving undecidability of non-trivial properties of r.e. sets.
Why included: Powerful generalization of undecidability. Widely applicable.

### The Busy Beaver Problem and the Meaning of Life
Author: Scott Aaronson
Year: 2016
Link: https://www.scottaaronson.com/papers/bb.pdf
Description: Modern analysis of the busy beaver function and its relationship to undecidability.
Why included: Connects classical undecidability to modern questions.

### Degrees of Unsolvability of Continuous Functions
Author: Marian Pour-El, Ian Richards
Year: 1983
Publisher: Journal of the ACM
DOI: 10.1145/322374.322387
Link: https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/322374.322387
Description: Extends undecidability to analysis and continuous mathematics.
Why included: Shows undecidability appears beyond discrete computation.

## CLASSIC

### A Note on the Entscheidungsproblem
Author: Alonzo Church
Year: 1936
Publisher: Journal of Symbolic Logic
Link: https://www.jstor.org/stable/2269326
Description: Church's independent proof of undecidability of first-order logic.
Why included: Parallel development to Turing. Shows robustness of undecidability.

### Undecidability and Intractability in Theoretical Physics
Author: Various authors
Year: 2012
Publisher: Physical Review Letters
Link: https://journals.aps.org/prl/
Description: Collection of papers showing undecidable problems in physics.
Why included: Undecidability has physical manifestations.
