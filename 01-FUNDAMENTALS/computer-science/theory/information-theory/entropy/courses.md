## BEGINNER

### Information Theory (MIT 6.441)
Instructor: Yury Polyanskiy
Platform: MIT OpenCourseWare
Year: 2016
Duration: Full semester (40 lectures)
Link: https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-441-information-theory-spring-2016/
Description: Comprehensive introduction covering entropy, source coding, channel capacity, and rate-distortion theory
Why included: Excellent video lectures with full materials, taught by leading expert

### Introduction to Information Theory
Instructor: Raymond Yeung
Platform: Coursera / Chinese University of Hong Kong
Year: 2020
Duration: 8 weeks
Link: https://www.coursera.org/learn/information-theory
Description: Accessible introduction with focus on fundamental concepts and applications
Why included: Well-structured for self-learners, includes practical examples

### Information Theory, Pattern Recognition, and Neural Networks
Instructor: David MacKay
Platform: University of Cambridge (Video Archive)
Year: 2012
Duration: 16 lectures
Link: http://videolectures.net/course_information_theory_pattern_recognition/
Description: Connects information theory to machine learning and pattern recognition
Why included: Integrates multiple perspectives, excellent lecturer, free access
