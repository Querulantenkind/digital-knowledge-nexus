## BEGINNER

### Introduction to Algorithms (CLRS)
Author: Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, Clifford Stein
Year: 2009 (3rd Edition)
Publisher: MIT Press
ISBN: 978-0262033848
Link: https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262033848/introduction-to-algorithms/
Description: Comprehensive textbook covering sorting algorithms including merge sort, quicksort, and heap sort with rigorous analysis.
Why included: The canonical reference for algorithm design; Chapter 2-3 introduce comparison-based sorting with detailed complexity analysis and proofs.

### Algorithm Design Manual
Author: Steven S. Skiena
Year: 2020 (3rd Edition)
Publisher: Springer
ISBN: 978-3030542559
Link: https://www.algorist.com/
Description: Practical guide to algorithm design with clear explanations of sorting algorithms and when to use each one.
Why included: Accessible presentation with practical insights and real-world considerations for sorting algorithm selection.

## INTERMEDIATE

### The Art of Computer Programming, Volume 3: Sorting and Searching
Author: Donald E. Knuth
Year: 1998 (2nd Edition)
Publisher: Addison-Wesley Professional
ISBN: 978-0201896839
Link: https://www-cs-faculty.stanford.edu/~knuth/taocp.html
Description: Monumental work providing exhaustive analysis of sorting algorithms, including detailed studies of insertion sort, bubble sort, merge sort, quicksort, and heap sort.
Why included: Definitive technical reference with mathematical rigor; still the standard for deep algorithmic analysis despite age.

### Algorithms in C: Parts 1-4 (Fundamentals, Data Structures, Sorting, Searching)
Author: Robert Sedgewick
Year: 1998 (3rd Edition)
Publisher: Addison-Wesley
ISBN: 978-0201314653
Link: https://algs4.cs.princeton.edu/
Description: Masterful explanation of sorting algorithms with implementations and visual demonstrations of algorithm behavior.
Why included: Exceptional pedagogical approach; illustrations and implementations make complex concepts tangible.

## ADVANCED

### Handbook of Sorting Algorithms: Fundamentals
Author: Isaac D. Scherson (Editor)
Year: 1990
Publisher: World Scientific
ISBN: 978-9971501686
Link: https://www.worldscientific.com/worldscibooks/10.1142/0896

## CLASSIC

### The Programming of Computers
Author: John W. Mauchly, John von Neumann, Kathleen McNulty Mauchly Antonelli
Year: 1946
Publisher: Various (historical documents)
Description: Early foundational work on computer programming that influenced subsequent algorithm design methodology.
Why included: Historical significance in establishing computer science as a discipline.
