### Gaussian Elimination is not Optimal
Author: Volker Strassen
Year: 1969
DOI: 10.1007/BF02165411
Link: https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/BF02165411
Description: Introduces Strassen's matrix multiplication algorithm using divide-and-conquer to achieve O(n^2.807).
Why included: Breakthrough result; demonstrated unexpected complexity improvements.

## ADVANCED

Year: 1980
DOI: 10.1007/BF01840440
Link: https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/BF01840440

### A Fast Fourier Transform Algorithm
Author: James W. Cooley, John W. Tukey
Year: 1965
DOI: 10.1090/S0025-5718-1965-0178586-1
Link: https://www.ams.org/journals/mcom/1965-19-090/S0025-5718-1965-0178586-1/
Description: Introduces FFT using divide-and-conquer to compute DFT in O(n log n).
Why included: Revolutionary algorithm; signal processing foundation.
