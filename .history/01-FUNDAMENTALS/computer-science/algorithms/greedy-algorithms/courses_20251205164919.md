## BEGINNER

### MIT OpenCourseWare — Introduction to Algorithms (6.006)
Institution: MIT
Link: https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-006-introduction-to-algorithms-fall-2011/
Description: Course content covering greedy algorithms in the context of standard algorithmic topics (scheduling, Huffman coding).
Why included: High-quality lecture notes and assignments; great starter course for algorithmic techniques.

### Coursera — Algorithms: Design and Analysis (Stanford / Tim Roughgarden)
Platform: Coursera
Link: https://www.coursera.org/learn/algorithms-part1
Description: Lectures and programming assignments covering greedy methods and other core algorithmic paradigms.
Why included: Structured course with clear explanations and exercises to build intuition.
