## BEGINNER

### Introduction to Algorithms (CLRS) - Matching Sections
Author: Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, Stein
Year: 2009 (3rd Ed.)
Publisher: MIT Press
Link: https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262033848/introduction-to-algorithms/
Description: Standard algorithms textbook with accessible treatments of basic matching and flow reductions.
Why included: Good first reference that places matching in broader algorithmic context.

### Algorithm Design (Kleinberg & Tardos)
Author: Jon Kleinberg, Éva Tardos
Year: 2005
Publisher: Pearson
Link: https://www.pearson.com/
Description: Problem-driven presentation including bipartite matching and reductions to network flow.
Why included: Clear problem-to-solution approach; useful for students starting with matching.

---

## INTERMEDIATE

### Matching Theory
Author: László Lovász, M.D. Plummer
Year: 1986
Publisher: North-Holland
Link: https://www.elsevier.com/
Description: Comprehensive monograph covering structural and algorithmic aspects of graph matchings.
Why included: Classic reference; deep coverage of matching theory and algorithms.

### Graph Theory Texts (Bondy & Murty)
Author: J.A. Bondy, U.S.R. Murty
Year: 2008
Publisher: Springer
Link: http://www.graph-theory.com/
Description: Broad graph theory coverage with chapters on matchings and applications.
Why included: Solid theoretical grounding and worked examples.

## ADVANCED

### Combinatorial Optimization: Algorithms and Complexity
Author: Christos H. Papadimitriou, Kenneth Steiglitz
Year: 1982
Publisher: Dover / Prentice Hall
Link: https://www.pearson.com/
Description: Covers combinatorial optimization topics including matching, matroids, and polyhedral approaches.
Why included: Connects matching algorithms to broader optimization theory.

### Recent Monographs and Surveys on Matching and Stable Matchings
Author: Various (research surveys)
Year: 2010-2024
Link: arXiv / journal surveys
Description: Modern surveys covering algorithmic improvements, approximate matchings, and application domains.
Why included: Keeps readers up to date with recent advances and applied perspectives.

### Research Collections on Matching Algorithms
Author: Multiple
Year: 2015-2024
Link: arXiv and conference proceedings
Description: Papers and edited volumes on fast matching algorithms, dynamic matching, and distributed approaches.
