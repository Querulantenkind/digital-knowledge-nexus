## BEGINNER

### "Paths, Trees and Flowers" — Edmonds (Blossom algorithm)
Author: Jack Edmonds
Year: 1965
Venue: Canadian Journal of Mathematics / Conference expositions
Link: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edmonds%27s_algorithm
Description: Seminal paper introducing polynomial-time algorithms for general graph matching (blossom algorithm).
Why included: Foundational algorithm for maximum matching in general graphs.

### Hopcroft–Karp algorithm (Bipartite Matching)
Author: John Hopcroft, Richard Karp
Year: 1973
Venue: SIAM Journal on Computing
Link: https://epubs.siam.org/
Description: Efficient algorithm for maximum bipartite matching with O(sqrt(V)E) runtime.
Why included: Standard algorithm taught in courses and used in practice for bipartite matching.

## INTERMEDIATE

### Hungarian Algorithm (Kuhn–Munkres) for Assignment
Author: Harold Kuhn / James Munkres
Year: 1955 / 1957
Venue: Operational Research literature
Link: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hungarian_algorithm
Description: Algorithm for assignment problems using matching techniques.
Why included: Important practical algorithm linking matching to optimization.
