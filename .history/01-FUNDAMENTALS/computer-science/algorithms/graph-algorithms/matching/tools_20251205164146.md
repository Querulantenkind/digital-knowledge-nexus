## BEGINNER

### NetworkX (Python) - Matching utilities
Creator: NetworkX developers
Year: 2005-Present
Platform: Python
Link: https://networkx.org/
Description: Functions for bipartite and general matchings, plus flow-based reductions.
Why included: Easy-to-use for prototyping and small-scale experiments.

## INTERMEDIATE

### Blossom V (Micali & Vazirani / Kolmogorov implementations)
Creator: Vladimir Kolmogorov and others
Year: 2000s-Present
Platform: C++ / GitHub repositories
Link: Search "Blossom V" or "Kolmogorov matching"
Description: High-performance implementation of Edmonds' blossom algorithm for general graphs.
Why included: Production-ready solver for exact maximum matchings.

Creator: Lemon contributors
