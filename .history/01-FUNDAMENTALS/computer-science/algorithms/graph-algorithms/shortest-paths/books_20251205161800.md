## BEGINNER

### Introduction to Algorithms (CLRS) - Chapters 24-25
Author: Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, Clifford Stein
Year: 2009 (3rd Edition)
Publisher: MIT Press
ISBN: 978-0262033848
Link: https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262033848/introduction-to-algorithms/
Description: Foundational chapters covering single-source and all-pairs shortest paths with Dijkstra's, Bellman-Ford, Floyd-Warshall algorithms and rigorous complexity analysis.
Why included: Canonical reference; clear exposition of core algorithms with proofs and practical considerations.

### Algorithm Design Manual (Chapters 6-7)
Author: Steven S. Skiena
Year: 2020 (3rd Edition)
Publisher: Springer
ISBN: 978-3030542559
Link: https://www.algorist.com/
Description: Practical treatment of shortest path algorithms with emphasis on when to use which algorithm and real-world applications.
Why included: Problem-solving oriented; excellent for understanding when algorithms apply.

Author: Donald E. Knuth
Year: 1998 (2nd Edition)
Publisher: Addison-Wesley Professional
ISBN: 978-0201896839
Link: https://www-cs-faculty.stanford.edu/~knuth/taocp.html
