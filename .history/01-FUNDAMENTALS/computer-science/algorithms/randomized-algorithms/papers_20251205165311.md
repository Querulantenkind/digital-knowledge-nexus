## BEGINNER

### Randomized quicksort and partitioning analyses
Author: Classic algorithm analyses (Hoare, later randomized analyses)

## INTERMEDIATE

### Karger's min-cut algorithm
Author: David Karger
Year: 1993
Venue: STOC/Journal versions
Description: Simple randomized contraction algorithm for global minimum cut with elegant analysis.
Why included: Clean randomized algorithm with surprising power and simple analysis.

### Rabin's fingerprinting and randomized hashing papers
Author: Michael O. Rabin and follow-ups
Description: Foundations of probabilistic hashing and randomized string algorithms.
Why included: Practical randomized techniques widely used in data structures and algorithms.
