### Suffix Trees and Their Applications
Author: Edward M. McCreight
Year: 1976
Description: Seminal paper introducing suffix trees and O(n) construction algorithm.
Why included: Foundational work; introduces data structure with immediate impact.

### A New Approach to Text Searching
Author: Peter Weiner
Year: 1973
Description: Early work on suffix trees; foundational construction algorithm.
Why included: Historical context; understanding evolution of suffix structures.

## INTERMEDIATE

### Suffix Arrays: A New Method for On-Line String Searches
Author: Udi Manber & Gene Myers
Year: 1990
Description: Introduces suffix arrays as space-efficient alternative to suffix trees.
Why included: Practical data structure; easier to implement and analyze.

### Linear-Time Construction of Suffix Arrays
Author: Juha Kärkkäinen & Peter Sanders
Year: 2003
Description: Efficient algorithms for suffix array construction.
Why included: Important for practical applications; optimal time complexity.
