## BEGINNER

### Introduction to Algorithms (CLRS) — Chapter 32 (String Algorithms)
Author: Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, Stein
Year: 2009 (3rd Edition)
Publisher: MIT Press
Link: [CLRS](https://mitpress.mit.edu/9780262033848/introduction-to-algorithms/)
Description: Introduction to suffix arrays and suffix trees with correctness proofs.
Why included: Authoritative reference; rigorous treatment of suffix data structures.

### Algorithm Design Manual (Chapter 8.4 — Suffix Trees)
Author: Steven S. Skiena
Year: 2020 (3rd Edition)
Publisher: Springer
Link: [Algorithm Design Manual](https://www.algorist.com/)
Description: Practical guidance on suffix trees and applications in string processing.
Why included: Problem-oriented perspective; when and why to use suffix structures.

## INTERMEDIATE

### Algorithms on Strings, Trees, and Sequences
Author: Dan Gusfield
Year: 1997
Publisher: Cambridge University Press
Link: [String Algorithms Book](https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/algorithms-on-strings-trees-and-sequences/)
